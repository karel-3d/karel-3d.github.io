Reply message Sheep Marketplace want to hear your wishes!
Dear users of sheep marketplace,

These past few days our programmers focused mainly on improving security, coding and adding new servers. This is not only for security and backup purposes, but also to be able survive in the event of attacks such as DDOS attempts. We feel that things are under control now, and will respond diligently as needed.

We understand that a lot of you are here, finding a new home after the loss of Silk Road and now even the loss of BMR. We want to hear what you have to say, but just keep in mind there is a small staff, a large userbase, and just an overloaded plate that just keeps filling up. Security is always a number one priority, so please accept our most sincere apologies if during anytime you feel that your request for some functionality or aesthetic feature has been ignored. Without complete security and peace of mind, even the best of functions serve no use.

So here are some questions to ponder, we will do our best to read through and respond where we can. Please keep in mind we are a very limited staff and are very busy. We are doing our best, but we will not cut any corners that will in anyway compromise our security. We understand that there may be some downtime due to this, but security must always be of the utmost importance.


What do you like/hate on Sheep Marketplace?



What functions would you like to see as a vendor/buyer?



Are you satisfied with our Technical Support and why?




You are welcome to add any other suggestions or toss us a comment, we really appreciate your feedback!

Thank you for your time.

So, I love the layout of the sheep marketplace and there are some things that can be done to enhance the experience as a buyer.

Firstly, my experience with tech support: I think support were quite slow to deal with the herbalist issue for buyers with BTC in escrow. There are many new buyers and vendors on here and it is important to build confidence that the site will help deal with disputes in a timely manner.

I would like to see removal of check box buttons on the orders page. If I change the quantity of an item and press update then the page should assume I want to change the quantity. The same with ordering except orders should require pin confirmation instead.

Next, on the vendor's page, there should be a table of all items for sale with the price next to it. Makes it easy to see what is for sale without having to view each item. It would also be useful to have a link next to each feedback to the item the buyer purchased.

Ability to select favourite vendors would be useful too but I read that this is under development.

Also a help section for newbies would be useful as right now all the help is for vendors. Tutorial to help people know how to use encryption and buy safely could be put together with help of knowledgeable forum users.

It would also be good if ability to filter domestic only was easier to find. Right now it is under account but it would be helpful to have it under the search too.

And finally some feedback on forums. Could we have search feature and also ability to add to posts (maybe remove text from posts too but but sure if this could cause danger if people can delete stuff.

I'm sure there are more useful things I could think of but basically I would like the site to be more streamlined and a bit easier to use for buyers as if you make it easier to use then it is easier for people to buy and make you more money :-)
