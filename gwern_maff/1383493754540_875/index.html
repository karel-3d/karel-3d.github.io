 Sheep FAQ - PLEASE READ Sat Oct 19, 2013 / 12:26 AM

Reply message Sheep FAQ - PLEASE READ
Hey Everyone

The Sheppard here, just want to lay down some frequently asked questions and our responses. Please give this a read and I will update it when I can to include more topics

1. Does Sheep utilize an escrow system?

Yes we do have an escrow system that is fully implemented on Sheep Marketplace. We strongly recommend that you don't finalize early except if you are willing to place you full trust in the seller! Once you have finalized it is not possible for us to provide any sort of support or compensation in the event of nondelivery; verified seizures not withstanding.

2. What is a Bitcoin? Where can they be purchased?

To make purchases on Sheep you need bitcoins (btc). There are several sites where these can be purchased. Some sites include mtgox.com, bitstamp.com, and localbitcoins.com to just name a few! For more info you can check some informative tutorials on Youtube.

3. How can I deposit (send) Bitcoins to my Sheep account?

The first step is to generate a deposit address. This can be found by selecting 'Account' (top left) and then 'Deposit'. From here you can generate new deposit addresses. There is a limit of one address per 24 hours. BTC can be sent to this deposit address and then be used to purchase items on Sheep.

4. How long does a deposit take to appear in my account?

The bitcoin network handles all bitcoin transactions, and as such the network takes several confirmations before a deposit will be available. Because of this bitcoins may not appear in your account for up to 24 hours. The typical deposit may take up to an hour, however there are exceptions. Please do not contact us until 24 hours has passed since your deposit was made. You can also check the status of your deposit on the blockchain. ie www.blockchain.info


5. I forgot my pin, why is there a 24 hour wait period?

The pin you entered when you registered provides additional security for your account. The 24 hour wait period ensures that if a hacker were to take over your account and attempt to reset your pin, you would know if you logged in within 24 hours. This is a set rule, and there are no exceptions to help ensure the security for all users of this site.


6. How do I cancel an order

Vendors can cancel order before it has been marked as approved. This is done by selecting the disapprove and delete option.
Customers can cancel under two different scenarios:
-Vendor hasn't marked order as approved in 3 days
-Vendor hasn't marked order as sent in 5 days


7. Seller or vendor doesn't communicate, or hasn't logged in for an extended period of time.

If the seller is non responsive and your order hasn't arrived you can file a complaint under your orders page and place a resolution offer to the vendor. If the vendor still doesn't respond for another 4 days, please contact Technical Support.
If a buyer has been unresponsive you must wait the 14 days period, at which point autofinalize will kick in and release the funds.

8. What is a PGP key

PGP (pretty good privacy) key is used for encrypted communication. You should ALWAYS use pgp to encrypt your address before you send it to your seller. Please see the forums, youtube, or google for more information and more detailed tutorials.

9. My vendor is banned but i have order with him! What should I do?

If you didn't finalize you are ok! Sheep will automatically give you back your BTC within 24 hours. If you have finalized unfortunately there is no support from Sheep, this is why when you finalize you are taking full responsibility.

10. I am seller, how long does it take to get verified.

You need to have 50 positive feedback sales to become verified. Sheep provides verification automatically, once a day.


11. What do I do if my seller requests me to finalize early?

We strongly recommend you use the escrow system. However there are many legitimate vendors who may require you to finalize, either because you are a new buyer or because they want to be able to sell the BTC before any major fluctuation occurs.
That being said, when you finalize you are PUTTING FULL TRUST IN THE SELLER. If your order does not arrive there is nothing sheep can do. Either tell the seller to cancel your order and go elsewhere, or put your trust in the seller and finalize. The choice is up to you, just know that nothing can be done once finalized.

12. Am I allowed to advertise other markerplaces on Sheep

We will allow the discussion of other marketplaces, but directly advertising other markerplaces are forbidden due to the fact that several of these places are scam attempts. We do not want to endorse people who are scamming bitcoins. Any direct advertisements for other markets will be deleted


There will be zero tolerance for any aggressiveness or any bad manners. Please act kindly be polite, and treat others with respect. We are all here for the same reason, to help obtain what oppression has prevented us from having access to. Play nice, and be fair and in turn this will be a great herd!

Thank you

Sheppard Out...
